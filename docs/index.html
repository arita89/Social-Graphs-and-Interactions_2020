The Music Galaxy
- Analysis of music network

World of music industry is vast and deeply interconnected. An ordinary song embarks on an amazing journey from an idea to the final streamable or physical medium. Before you can enjoy your newest release of Ariana Grande or Drake, the song needs to be written both in terms of music composition and lyrics. Then it has to be recorded, which requires a collaborative effort of the primary artist, session musicians or members of the band, as well as the engineer responsible for recording the material. This step is usually done with the help from the record label the artist belongs to. Finally, before the song makes into a single, EP or LP, it needs to fine-tuned by a mastering engineer, who processes the audio single for the best possible audio experience.

In our work we would like dive deep into this complicated process, by investigating the connections between songs and most important people, such as: primary artists, writers, producers, engineers and performers, who are responsible for delivering our favorite music. We encourage you to follow the suggested order of chapters, starting from the complex and vast network, through the communities of artists, who often work together and finally investigating in detail two artists, who despite being from different decades and genres are still connected through just a couple of artists.
